The Raymond Corporation History

The Raymond Corporation, which has locations all over the country but began in Greene, is a manufacturer that specializes in creating industrial equipment such as forklifts and pallet jacks.

The company has played a significant role in the local economy and has been a main employer in the Greene area for many years.

The company, which actually hires from RIT specifically, often participates in job fairs and looks for students majoring in engineering and other technology fields.

Roots of the Company

George Raymond Sr. founded the Raymond Corporation in 1922.

The company was located in the village of Greene, meaning it was close to the Chenango River and the railroad, which were both important for transportation of goods at the time.

Over the years, the company has expanded to where it is today.

A Part of Toyota

Within the last couple of decades, The Raymond Corporation has merged with Toyota Material Handling.

This means that Raymond's products have their reach all over the globe, not just nationally.

  • Fun Fact: Raymond-branded forklifts can be seen in popular shows like Breaking Bad!
