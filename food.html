Restaurants & Food Greene, NY

While Greene is a small town, it offers big flavors with a wide variety of restaurants and food options. From classic American diners to Italian restaurants, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Featured: Spiedies

A Binghamton original, Spiedies are chunks of chicken or pork grilled in a special marinade, usually served on a roll.

An entire festival each August celebrates this local meal.

Tavern 1244 at Baron's Inn

Tavern 1244 at Baron's Inn is a fancier dining spot in Greene. The menu features a variety of options, including classic American dishes, local specialties, as well as entrees.

Popular menu items:

  • Salmon - A honey-glazed piece of salmon served on a wooden maple board. Sides include seasoned rice and steamed vegetables.
  • Ribeye - A hardy piece of steak full of flavor, usually served with mashed potatoes and your choice of vegetables.
  • Grilled Chicken Sandwich - A great lunchtime choice full of flavor and sweet pickles. Served with fries or chips.

Magro's Italian Restaurant

A family owned and operated restaurant, Magro's specializes in Italian cuisine. A great choice if you are feeling a hankering for a pepperoni pizza or spaghetti and meatballs.

Popular menu items:

  • Specialty Pizzas - From regular cheese to chicken bacon ranch, there are tons of options for slices of pizza.
  • Chicken Parmesan - A large breaded chicken cutlet topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese, served with a side of pasta.
  • Spaghetti and Meatballs - A large portion of angle hair pasta and meatballs, topped with marinara sauce.

Boudreau's Bistro

The classic American diner- serves breakfast, as well as a wide variety of sandwiches, burgers, wraps, and more. Also an excellent spot for ice cream, as they have over a hundred flavors of ice cream and custard to choose from.

Popular menu items:

  • Sausage Gravy On A Biscuit - Two large biscuits drowned in gravy mixed with sausage bits.
  • Volcano Burger - A large burger with melted cheese on the inside, topped with glazed onions, lettuce, and tomato.
  • The Rachel - A hot turkey sandwich and swiss cheese.
